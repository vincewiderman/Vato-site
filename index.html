About VATO

I'm Vince Widerman. I DJ as VATO.

I'm an open-format DJ in Morgantown, West Virginia — the resident DJ at Ivy Nightclub and Joe Mamas, and the assistant entertainment manager at Ivy. Most of my work is live nightlife crowds, which is exactly what makes me different at events. I've learned how a room actually moves, so I'm not guessing when the energy needs to shift.

Open-format means I'm not stuck in one lane — rap, house, throwbacks, country, Latin, pop, mixed live to whatever the night calls for. For private and formal events, that same instinct comes with preparation: clear communication, professional mics, and the right volume for the room.

My engineering background shapes how I approach every booking — every part of the night planned in advance, every transition prepared, nothing left to chance.

The setup: Pioneer DDJ-FLX10 · dual JBL EON 715 mains with EON 718S subwoofer · wireless mic system + Shure SM58 · professional stands and cabling.
